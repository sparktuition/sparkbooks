# SparkBooks - Available Routes

## Main Pages
- `/` - Home page (all sections combined)
- `/retreat` - Writers' Retreat landing page

## Individual Section Routes

### Home Page Sections
- `/stats` - Literary Excellence Statistics
- `/retreat-overview` - Retreat Overview Section
- `/about` - About Section
- `/timeline` - Timeline Section
- `/books` - Featured Books Section
- `/collection` - Collection Section
- `/author` - Author Details Section
- `/collections-page` - Collections Page Section
- `/services` - Author Services Section
- `/social` - Social Presence Section
- `/testimonials` - Testimonials Section
- `/events` - Upcoming Events Section
- `/press` - Press Coverage Section
- `/behind-scenes` - Behind The Scenes Section
- `/contact` - Contact Section

## Retreat Page Individual Section Routes

### Retreat Sections
- `/retreat/hero` - Retreat Hero Landing
- `/retreat/overview` - Retreat Overview (Two Column)
- `/retreat/extraordinary` - What Makes It Special
- `/retreat/features` - Features & Amenities
- `/retreat/audience` - Who Should Attend
- `/retreat/mentors` - Meet the Mentors
- `/retreat/itinerary` - Schedule & Timeline
- `/retreat/daily` - Daily Experiences
- `/retreat/activities` - Special Activities
- `/retreat/accommodation` - Accommodation Details (Villa)
- `/retreat/stay` - Resort Experience & Payment
- `/retreat/cuisine` - Food & Dining
- `/retreat/essentials` - What to Bring & Guidelines
- `/retreat/seats` - Limited Seats & Pricing

- `/retreat/cta` - Final Call to Action

## Error Handling
- Any undefined route (e.g., `/invalid`) - Shows 404 page with link to home

## Usage
Users can now access any section directly via its corresponding route, making it easy to bookmark, share, or link to specific pages.

### Examples:
**Home Page Sections:**
- `https://sparkbooks.com/books` - Go directly to Featured Books section
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat-overview` - Retreat overview on home page
- `https://sparkbooks.com/contact` - Go directly to Contact section

**Retreat Page Sections:**
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat` - Full retreat page (all sections)
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat/features` - Go directly to Features & Amenities
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat/mentors` - Go directly to Mentors section
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat/seats` - Go directly to Pricing & Seats section
- `https://sparkbooks.com/retreat/itinerary` - Go directly to Schedule & Timeline
